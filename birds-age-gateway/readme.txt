== Description ==

Birds Age Gateway is an age verification plugin.

Whatever your reasons, you may need your siteâ€™s visitors to confirm their age before viewing the content of your site. This plugin allows you to have full control on a restriction gateway.

This plugin is Localization/Internationalization ready and follows WordPress i18n standards.


== Installation ==

1. Unzip plugin files and upload them under your '/wp-content/plugins/' directory.
2. Resulted names will be: '/wp-content/plugins/birds-age-gateway/'
3. Activate plugin at "Plugins" administration page.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I add my own logo? =

Yes, and a lot more to brand your restriction page.

= Do you save any cookies in order not to show the restriction page twice? =

Yes, and it's configurable.

= Can I change the date format? =

Yes! DD MM YYYY and MM DD YYYY are supported.
